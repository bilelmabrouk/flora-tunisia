Annual phlox

Phlox drummondii  ·  Family: Polemoniaceae
Exterior · Flowering / bedding
EN Annual phlox  ·  FR Phlox  ·  AR فلوكس  ·  Phlox drummondii
Origin: Texas (south-central USA)
Light
Full sun
Water
Low
Care
Easy
Safety
Pet & child safe
Setting
Outdoor
In short A cheerful, low-growing annual producing a dense carpet of starry flowers in pink, red, purple and white. Best in Tunisian spring and autumn — it struggles in peak summer heat but rewards with little attention.
Light
Full sun; tolerates light afternoon shade in summer.
Water
Moderate; do not let soil dry out completely; avoid overwatering.
Soil
Well-drained, fertile, slightly acidic to neutral.
Temperature
Cool-season performer; best 15–22 °C; sow in autumn for spring bloom in Tunisia.
Humidity
Average; adequate airflow reduces powdery mildew.
Feeding
Light balanced fertilizer every 3–4 weeks during active growth.
Pruning & grooming
Deadhead spent flowers regularly; shear back by one-third after the first flush.
Propagation
From seed; sow indoors 6–8 weeks before planting out or direct-sow in mild weather.
Mature size
15–40 cm tall; spreading habit.
Growth rate
Moderate in cool weather; fast-flowering.
Lifespan
Annual.
Bloom / fruit season
Spring and autumn (March–June and September–October in Tunisia).
Hardiness (min temp)
Tolerates light frost to about −4 °C.
Toxicity & safety
Non-toxic to humans and pets.
Fertilizer (Tunisia) A light balanced fertilizer every 3–4 weeks during cool growing periods encourages compact, floriferous plants; avoid heavy feeding in warm weather.  Find fertilizer →

Troubleshooting
SymptomLikely causeWhat to do
Powdery mildew — white coating on leavesPoor air circulation, warm humid nights.Space plants well; avoid wetting foliage; remove affected leaves.
Plants stop flowering in midsummerHeat stress above 28 °C — natural summer pause.Shear back lightly; provide afternoon shade; plants resume when temperatures drop.
Root rot — plants wilt and die rapidlyWaterlogged or poorly drained soil.Improve drainage; water only when top of soil feels dry; replace in fresh compost.
Availability: Seed packets and seedlings available in Tunisian garden centres in spring and autumn.
