Persimmon

Diospyros kaki  ·  Family: Ebenaceae
Trees · Fruit / nut
EN Persimmon  ·  FR Plaqueminier (kaki)  ·  AR كاكي  ·  Diospyros kaki
Origin: China and East Asia
Light
Full sun
Water
Low
Care
Moderate
Safety
Pet & child safe
Setting
Outdoor
In short A handsome deciduous tree grown for its sweet orange autumn fruit, well-adapted to northern and central Tunisia. The striking ornamental value in autumn makes it a dual-purpose garden tree.
Light
Full sun for best fruiting and autumn colour; tolerates light afternoon shade.
Water
Moderate; regular watering during establishment and fruit development; drought-tolerant once mature.
Soil
Well-drained, deep, fertile loam; tolerates a range of soil types including slightly calcareous.
Temperature
Hardy to −15 °C depending on variety; needs 100–400 chilling hours in winter.
Humidity
Average; tolerates Tunisian coastal and continental conditions.
Feeding
Balanced fertilizer in early spring; low-nitrogen formula to avoid excess vegetative growth.
Pruning & grooming
Light pruning to maintain open crown; remove crossing or dead branches in late winter.
Propagation
By grafting onto seedling rootstock; seed-grown trees are variable and slow.
Mature size
4–8 m tall; can be kept smaller with regular pruning.
Growth rate
Moderate; begins fruiting from year 4–6.
Lifespan
Long-lived — 50–100 years.
Bloom / fruit season
Small, inconspicuous flowers in April–May; bold orange fruit in October–December.
Hardiness (min temp)
Hardy in northern and central Tunisia; frost-tender varieties may struggle in region 2.
Toxicity & safety
Fruit is safe and nutritious; unripe astringent fruit is unpleasant but not toxic.
Fertilizer (Tunisia) Apply a balanced fruit-tree fertilizer in early spring; switch to a low-nitrogen, phosphorus-rich formula as fruit develops to support fruit quality.  Find fertilizer →

Black sooty mould on leavesScale insect or mealybug honeydew deposits.Control scale/mealybug with horticultural oil spray; wipe off mould with damp cloth.
Astringent fruit even when fully colouredAstringent variety — needs full ripening on tree or off-tree conditioning.Leave fruit on tree until after first cold spell or store with ripe apples to release ethylene.
Availability: Available in nurseries mainly in northern and central Tunisia; less common in the south.
