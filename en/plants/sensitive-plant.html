Sensitive plant (touch-me-not)

Mimosa pudica  ·  Family: Fabaceae
Interior · Foliage
EN Sensitive plant (touch-me-not)  ·  FR Sensitive (mimosa pudique)  ·  AR المستحية (لا تلمسني)  ·  Mimosa pudica
Origin: Tropical Americas
Light
Sun / part shade
Water
Moderate
Care
Easy
Safety
Pet & child safe
Setting
Indoor & out
In short A novelty plant whose feathery leaves fold shut instantly when touched; loved by children, it grows fast from seed and makes fluffy pink flower puffs.
Light
Bright light to part shade.
Water
Moderate; keep lightly moist.
Soil
Well-drained soil.
Temperature
Warm; tender to frost.
Humidity
Likes some humidity.
Feeding
Light feed in growth.
Pruning & grooming
Pinch to keep bushy.
Propagation
From seed, very easy.
Mature size
30–60 cm tall.
Growth rate
Fast.
Lifespan
Short-lived; often grown as an annual.
Bloom / fruit season
Pink puffs in summer.
Hardiness (min temp)
Not frost-hardy.
Toxicity & safety
Non-toxic; spines can prick.
Fertilizer (Tunisia) A light feed in growth is plenty; it grows readily from seed.  Find fertilizer →
