Common ivy ⇄ also: Interior

Hedera helix  ·  Family: Araliaceae
Exterior · Climber
EN Common ivy  ·  FR Lierre  ·  AR لبلاب  ·  Hedera helix
Origin: Europe and western Asia (native)

Setting
Indoor & out
In short The classic evergreen wall-coverer and shade-tolerant ground cover — one of the most versatile plants for northern Tunisian gardens, and also grows as a tough indoor trailing plant.
Light
Shade to part shade outdoors; also tolerates full shade. Brighter spots for variegated types.
Water
Low to moderate; drought-tolerant once established. Do not overwater.
Soil
Any well-drained soil; tolerates poor, dry, and heavy conditions.
Temperature
Hardy to −20 °C; grows well in northern and highland Tunisia.
Humidity
Adapts to a wide range; tolerates dry indoor air as a houseplant.
Feeding
Light balanced feed in spring; does not need much.
Pruning & grooming
Trim to keep boundaries; aggressive pruning is fine and rejuvenates the plant.
Propagation
Stem cuttings root very easily in water or soil at any season.
Mature size
Spreads indefinitely; control by regular trimming.
Growth rate
Moderate to fast.
Lifespan
Very long-lived.
Bloom / fruit season
September–November; small yellow-green flowers attractive to pollinators; black berries follow.
Hardiness (min temp)
Very hardy to −20 °C.
Toxicity & safety
Berries and leaves toxic if eaten; sap causes contact dermatitis — wear gloves.
Fertilizer (Tunisia) A light balanced fertilizer once in spring is enough; over-feeding produces soft growth prone to pests.  Find fertilizer →

Troubleshooting
SymptomLikely causeWhat to do
Spider mites in hot dry conditionsHeat stress, especially on indoor plants.Increase humidity; spray leaves with water; use insecticidal soap if severe.
Leaf spot diseaseFungal infection in wet, poorly ventilated conditions.Improve air circulation; remove affected leaves; avoid wetting foliage.
Variegated leaves reverting to greenInsufficient light for variegated cultivars.Move to brighter position; remove all-green shoots to preserve variegation.
Availability: Widely available in Tunisian nurseries in both outdoor and indoor varieties.
