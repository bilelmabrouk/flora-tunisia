Satin pothos

Scindapsus pictus  ·  Family: Araceae
Interior · Foliage
EN Satin pothos  ·  FR Scindapsus argenté  ·  AR سكيندابسوس  ·  Scindapsus pictus
Origin: South-East Asia (Borneo, Java, Philippines)
Light
Sun / part shade
Water
Low
Care
Easy
Safety
Toxic if eaten
Setting
Indoor
In short A trailing indoor vine with dark-green, silver-spotted leaves — easy to grow and looks stunning on a shelf or in a hanging pot. Tolerates lower light and occasional missed waterings.
Light
Bright to medium indirect light; the silver markings fade in very low light.
Water
Allow the top half of soil to dry before watering; about every 7–10 days in summer.
Soil
Well-draining peat-free potting mix; good drainage is essential to avoid root rot.
Temperature
15–30 °C; keep away from cold draughts and below 12 °C.
Humidity
Prefers moderate to high humidity; tolerates average conditions but mist in dry Tunisian summers.
Feeding
Every 4 weeks in spring–summer with a balanced diluted fertilizer.
Pruning & grooming
Pinch or trim long vines to keep bushy; pruned stems root easily in water.
Propagation
Stem cuttings with a node in water or moist soil; roots in 2–3 weeks.
Mature size
Trailing to 1–2 m long.
Growth rate
Moderate to fast in good conditions.
Lifespan
Long-lived with regular trimming and repotting.
Bloom / fruit season
Does not flower indoors.
Hardiness (min temp)
Not frost-hardy; minimum 12 °C.
Toxicity & safety
Toxic if ingested (calcium oxalates); irritant to mouth and throat — keep from children and pets.
Fertilizer (Tunisia) A balanced liquid fertilizer every 4 weeks in the growing season is enough; excess feeding causes salt build-up and brown leaf tips.  Find fertilizer →

Troubleshooting
SymptomLikely causeWhat to do
Pale or washed-out silver markingsToo little light.Move to a brighter spot with indirect light.
Yellow leaves with mushy stemsRoot rot from overwatering.Remove from pot, trim rotted roots, replant in fresh well-draining soil.
Brown crispy leaf edgesDry air or underwatering.Water more regularly and increase humidity around the plant.
Availability: Available in most Tunisian indoor plant shops; occasionally found online.
