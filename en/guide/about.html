How to use this guide

Each plant page follows the same layout so you can scan it fast. The icon strip at the top shows light, water, temperature, and difficulty at a glance. Below it, the care fields cover watering, soil, feeding, and pruning for our climate. A troubleshooting box lists the problems people actually hit here, with plain fixes.

At the bottom of the page you will find the region block, showing which of the 6 Tunisian zones suit the plant outdoors, and a QR code linking to more photos and updates. A crossover chip means the plant crosses over between two uses, for example a houseplant that also thrives on a shaded balcony, or an ornamental that is also edible.

Safety note: some plants listed as edible or medicinal can be harmful in the wrong dose, wrong part, or if confused with a look-alike. Treat those pages as background reading, not medical advice, and keep berries, bulbs, and sap away from children and pets unless you are certain of the identification.
