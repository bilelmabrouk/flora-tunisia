Seasonal calendar

SeasonWhat to do
SpringMain planting season across most of Tunisia. Sow and transplant while soil is moist and temperatures are mild, start feeding as growth resumes, and watch for the first aphids and snails on tender new shoots.
SummerSurvival mode in most regions: water deeply in early morning or evening, add mulch to keep roots cool, and give afternoon shade to young or potted plants. Hold off on major pruning and heavy feeding during the hottest weeks.
AutumnA second good planting window as heat eases, especially for trees, shrubs, and perennials. Clear summer debris, refresh mulch, and divide or move perennials before the cooler, wetter months set in.
WinterRainy season in the north, mild but dry further south. Cut back watering for most plants, protect frost-sensitive species in the North-West mountains and cold nights inland, and use the slower pace for pruning and planning next year's garden.
Bilel Mabrouk & Claude
