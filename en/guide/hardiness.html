Hardiness & climate-zone key

Tunisia spans mild coasts to frosty north-western mountains and hot Saharan oases. Match a plant's 'Hardiness (min temp)' on its page to your zone below. Frost-tender plants (min temp near 0 °C) need protection in zones 2 and often 4.

Region-groupMin tempWhat it means
1 · Northern / Tell coast≈ 2 to 5 °CMild, humid; light frost rare. Suits most Mediterranean and tender plants.
2 · North-West / mountains≈ -5 to 0 °CColdest, wettest; real winter frost and occasional snow. Choose hardy plants.
3 · Sahel / Central-East≈ 4 to 7 °CMild coastal, semi-arid; frost very rare. Great for citrus and olives.
4 · Central interior / Steppe≈ -3 to 2 °CContinental, dry; cold nights and frost risk plus summer heat. Tough plants only.
5 · South-East / Pre-desert≈ 5 to 9 °CArid, mild winters, very hot summers. Palms, olives, drought-lovers.
6 · South-West / Saharan oases≈ 4 to 8 °CHot, very dry; oasis shade helps. Date palm country; heat-proof species.
